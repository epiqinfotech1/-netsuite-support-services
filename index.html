Oracle's Built-in Tiers

Understanding Oracle's NetSuite Support Levels

Oracle provides several built-in support tiers with every NetSuite subscription. Basic Support includes SuiteAnswers, community forums, and standard ticket submission. Premium, Gold, and Platinum tiers offer prioritized case queuing, telephone support, and 24/7 critical coverage.

Beyond standard support, Oracle offers Advanced Customer Support (ACS) — a proactive model with five tiers: Advise, Monitor, Optimize, Architect, and Operate.

While Oracle's native support handles platform-level issues, most businesses need a certified NetSuite partner like EPIQ Infotech for faster, more personalized support that understands their specific business processes, customizations, and integrations.
